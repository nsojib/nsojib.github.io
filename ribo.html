Ribo. Social Humanoid Robot Wikipedia

Motivation: To make the fascination into a reality, I teamed up with robot enthusiasts and formed a robotics club named RoboSUST. Thanks to the excellent teamwork and experience from previous projects, we developed this robot within a month after getting a hundred thousand BDT sponsorship (~1200$) from the Bangladesh Science Fiction Society. We are fortunate to have a famous science fiction writer and a professor of CS dept. Dr. M. Zafar Iqbal as our supervisor in this project. We participated in the science-fiction festival where people were amazed to see such an artificial life talking and responding. Later, we participated in four exhibitions across the country. It's great fun to work in a team to achieve the same goal.
Research & Publication Robot's appearance and how it responds to people are vital criteria for a robot to be considered a social robot. This robot instantly responds by turning its head toward the direction it is being called by name. It can make short conversations and perform various motion to entertain humans. Its engaging behavior makes people feel supported to interact with it. In all the five public exhibitions across the country, we survey social robots, and 64.6% of people showed interest in getting this robot home, where 80.7% of people agreed that such robots would be suitable for working in home and office environments.
